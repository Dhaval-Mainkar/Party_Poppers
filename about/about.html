About Us

Small team. Big moments. A lifetime of happiness. With more than twelve years of experience planning wedding and events, we're experts in perfecting the details and creating the extraordinary. We exist so that you can enjoy the fun parts of planning and leave the stress behind. Our clients are intensely busy professionals who depend on us to make their planning process stress-free and to discover what will make their day uniquely special. We make design and planning simple for our clients - and simple isn't easy.

Born at the intersection of clever and creative, Party Poppers Event Planning harnesses the power of emotion to make your most important day less stressful and more beautiful. At our core, we are a culture of action and imagination

Party Poppers Event Planning was founded in 2008. We have offices in both Mumbai and Delhi, which service local destinations. Over the years, we've been honored to have been featured in places like DNA, CNN, The New York Times, The Knot, The Today Show, Carats and Cake, Brides, The Big Fat Weddings, Modern Luxury Weddings, and more!
